The 5 R's

The popular Zero Waste Movement shares 5 R's of sustainability:

  1. Refuse: say no to what you do not need
  2. Reduce: reduce the amount of waste you create
  3. Reuse: use things again and again
  4. Recycle: recycle what you cannot reuse
  5. Rot: compost remaining biodegradable waste
